1. What are different ways to style your form?

You can style your form by changing colors, fonts, borders, spacing, input sizes, button styles.

2. How does flex help for both Desktop and Mobile phones?

Flex helps by putting form items in a line or column that can change size. This makes the form look good on big screens (desktop) and small screens (mobile).
