3.What are special entity characters?

Special Entity Characters are used to display characters that have special meaning in HTML like:

The "less than sign":
5 + 10 < 30
The "greater than sign"
55 > 10 + 20

4.What are phrase tags?

Phrase tags are used to make a group of word to stand out, like the Bold text or the Italic text.

5.What is the difference between OL and UL Tags?

When order matter the "ol" tag is used to create a numbered list, but when the order doesn't matter the "ul" tag is used to create a bullet list or an unordered list (see example at the top of the page).

6.What is a DL?

In HTML "dl" means "definition list" and it is used to lists terms and their definitions or description (see example at the top of the page).
