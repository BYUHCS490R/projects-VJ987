Answers

What goes in the header?

Usually, the website's logo, brand will go in the "header".

What goes in the Nav? Can the Nav go in the header? Does it have to go in the header? Does the Nav contain links to another website?

The "nav" tag contains links that help you move around a website, like Home, About, or Contact.The "nav" tag can go in the "header" tag at the top of the page, but it doesn't have to be. It can also include links to other websites, not just pages on your own site.

What goes in Main?

The "main " tag contain the main content of the the website, like text, images, or article that are important for the people to see.

What goes in the Footer?

This section is at the bottom of the page and usually contain things like copyright infornation, contact details, or social media icons.
