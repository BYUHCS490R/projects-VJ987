Answers

1. What is the difference between block tags and inline tags?

The block tags like "p", "header" or "h1" start on a new line and take the full width of the page while the inline tags, like "span", "a" or "strong" only takes the space they need.

2. What is the margin, border, and padding?

The margin is the space outside the element, the border is the line around the element and the padding is the space between the border and the content. It basically control how much space is around things on the page.

3. What are units of measurement you can use in CSS?

You can use pixels (px) as fixed size, you can also use percent (%) but you can also other measurements like Em and rem, or Vw and vh. Finally you can use inches(in) or centimeters (cm).

4. When would you use a figure tag?

This tag keeps the image and caption together.
