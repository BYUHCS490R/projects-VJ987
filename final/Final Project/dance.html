Ori Tahiti

Traditional Tahitian dances are called Ori Tahiti. They are performed during celebrations and festivals. Dances express stories and emotions of the people.

Music uses drums and other instruments to create rhythm for the dances. Learning these dances helps us understand Tahitian culture and values.
