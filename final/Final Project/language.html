Learn Basic Tahitian

Here are some common Tahitian phrases used in daily life:

Common Tahitian Phrases
Tahitian English
Ia ora na Hello
Manava Welcome
Haere mai Come here
Eaha to oe i'oa? What is your name?
O .......... to'u i'oa My name is ..........
No hea mai oe? Where are you from?
No .......... mai au I am from ..........
Eaha to oe huru? How are you?
Maita'i Good
Mauruuru Thank you
Goodbye Nana

Learning these phrases helps you understand Tahitian culture and communicate respectfully with locals.
