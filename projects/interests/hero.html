Batman

Batman, aka Bruce Wayne, is one of the most iconic superheroes in history. Unlike other heroes, he has no superpower. Instead, he relies on his intelligence, martialart knowledge, detective skills and advanced technology (and MONEY). What you can learn from Batman is that trus strength can come from determination and courage rather than superhuman abilities.

Gadgets
Utility belt, Batarangs, Batmobile, Grapple hook, and more high-tech tools.
Allies
Robin, Alfred Pennyworth, Comissioner Gordon, and the Justice League.
Famous Movies
Batman Begins (2005), The Dark Knight (2008), The Batman (2022).
