The bloodline of the Force

The Skywalker family is a very important family in the Star Wars movies. They are strong with the Force and help change the galaxy. The family starts with Anakin Skywalker, who later becomes Darth Vader, and Padmé Amidala, who is kind and brave. Their children, Luke and Leia, grow up to be heroes who fight for peace. The Skywalkers have many problems but always try to do what is right and bring hope to others.
